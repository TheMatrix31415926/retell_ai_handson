#### Appointment Booking and Rescheduling Flow



##### **Service Type**

Clinic Appointment Scheduling



##### **Greeting Message**

Hello! I’m your scheduling assistant. I can help you book, reschedule, or cancel your appointment quickly and easily. How can I assist you today?



##### **Booking Flow**

1. Please tell me your preferred date for the appointment.
2. What time works best for you on that date?
3. Great! I have scheduled your appointment on the selected date and time. Would you like to confirm this booking?



##### **Rescheduling Flow**

1. Sure, I can help you reschedule your appointment. Please tell me your new preferred date.
2. What time would you like on that date?
3. Your appointment has been updated to the new date and time. Would you like to confirm the changes?



##### **Cancellation Flow**

1. I understand you’d like to cancel your appointment.
2. Your appointment has been successfully cancelled.
3. If you need to book again in the future, feel free to reach out. Have a great day!



##### 

This flow ensures a smooth experience for booking, rescheduling, and cancellation while keeping the conversation clear and easy to follow.
