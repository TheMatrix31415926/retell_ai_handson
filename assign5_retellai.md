##### **Basic Customer Support Resolution Flow**





###### **Selected Issue:**

Login Trouble





###### **Greeting Message**

Hello! I'm here to help you with your login issue. Let's get this sorted out quickly.





###### **Diagnostic Questions**

1\. Are you receiving any error message when trying to log in? If yes, please specify.

2\. Have you recently changed your password?

3\. Are you trying to log in from a new device or browser?



###### **Automated Resolution Message**

It looks like this issue can be resolved automatically.  

Please try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" option.  

After resetting, log in again using your new credentials.  

If the issue was due to incorrect login details, this should fix it.





###### **Escalation Message**

Thanks for your responses. This issue needs further assistance from our support team.  

I’m transferring your request to a human agent who will help you shortly.  

Please stay connected while we get this resolved for you.
