##### **HR Screening Conversation Flow**





**Role Chosen: Junior Frontend Developer**





##### **Opening Message**



"Hi there! I'm Aria, an AI screening assistant. I'll be asking you a few quick questions for the Junior Frontend Developer role you applied for. This should only take about 5 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers — just be yourself. Ready? Let's begin!







##### Screening Questions In Numbered Order



**1. Background Intro**



"To get started, could you briefly tell me about your background? For example, what you studied or how you got into frontend development?"

**Capture:** Education background, how candidate entered the field (self-taught / degree / bootcamp)





**2. Experience \& Tech Stack**



"How many years have you been working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript? And are there any frameworks like React or Vue that you've used?"

**Capture:** Years of experience (0–1 / 1–2 / 2–3), specific frameworks known (React, Vue, Angular, etc.)





**3. Project or Work History**



"Can you describe one project you've built or contributed to? It could be a personal project, freelance work, or from a previous job."

**Capture:** Type of project (personal / freelance / professional), tech used, candidate's specific role in it





**4. Tools \& Workflow**



"What tools do you typically use day-to-day? For example, Git for version control, VS Code, Figma for designs, or anything else?"

**Capture:** Version control knowledge (Git/no Git), code editor, design handoff tools, any CI/CD awareness





**5. Availability \& Notice Period**



"If selected, when would you be available to start? Are you currently employed, and if so, how long is your notice period?"

**Capture:** Start availability (immediate / 2 weeks / 1 month+), currently employed (yes/no), notice period duration





**6. Location \& Work Preference**



"Finally, are you open to working from our office, or do you prefer a remote or hybrid setup? And are you located near \[City Name] or are you open to relocation?"

**Capture:** Work mode preference (remote / hybrid / on-site), current city, open to relocation (yes/no)





##### **Closing Message**



"That's all from my side — thank you so much for your time! Our HR team will review your responses and reach out within 2–3 business days. Have a great day!"
